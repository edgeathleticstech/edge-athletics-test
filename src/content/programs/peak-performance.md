## Program Features

- Skills training: shooting, ball handling, footwork
- Controlled scrimmages: competitive, high-intensity play
- Strength & conditioning: basketball-specific
- Varsity-level practices: structured sessions, modified for younger groups

## Who It's For

Athletes with prior basketball experience preparing for high school and competitive play. *This is not a beginner program.*

## Session Structure

Each session includes three segments:
1. **Train** — Strength and conditioning
2. **Practice** — Skill development and varsity-style drills
3. **Scrimmage** — Live play to apply new skills
